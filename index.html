All Ranks

Guardsman

Basic soldier rank in Guards regiments.

Performs section and platoon duties.

Lance Corporal

Junior NCO assisting section command.

Assists in training, leads small teams.

Lance Sergeant

Guards equivalent of Corporal, commands a section.

Commands a section, responsible for minor admin and training.

Sergeant

Senior NCO responsible for discipline and training.

Maintains discipline, oversees training of soldiers.

Colour Sergeant

Experienced NCO in charge of company-level administration.

Handles admin and logistics for the company.

Warrant Officer Class 2

Senior NCO at company or battalion level.

Advises officers, manages senior NCO duties.

Warrant Officer Class 1

Most senior NCO in the regiment.

Top NCO, regiment-wide responsibility.

Second Lieutenant

Junior commissioned officer leading a platoon.

Leads a platoon, supports company operations.

Lieutenant

Commands a platoon or supports company ops.

Leads platoon missions, assists Captain in company duties.

Captain

Company-level officer supporting tactical operations.

Responsible for company-level operations and training.

Major

Company commander or staff officer.

Commands company or serves on battalion staff.

Lieutenant Colonel

Battalion commanding officer.

Leads battalion and oversees all companies.

Rank Descriptions

Guardsman – Basic soldier performing section and platoon duties.

Lance Corporal – Junior NCO assisting in training and section leadership.

Lance Sergeant – Commands a section, equivalent of Corporal in Guards.

Sergeant – Senior NCO managing discipline and training.

Colour Sergeant – Experienced NCO responsible for company administration.

Warrant Officer Class 2 – Senior NCO at company or battalion level.

Warrant Officer Class 1 – Most senior NCO in the regiment.

Second Lieutenant – Junior commissioned officer, leads platoon.

Lieutenant – Platoon commander or supports company ops.

Captain – Company-level officer supporting tactical operations.

Major – Company commander or staff officer.

Lieutenant Colonel – Battalion commanding officer.
